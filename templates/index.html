SUperBiology

Is a free set of web tools created to help students learn the big ideas of modern biomolecular science. Each tool and simulation comes with a example tutorial/lesson plan that may be useful to faculty. Each also is designed to be customizable for open ended exploration and pedagogy. These tools were created by bioinformatics faculty and undergraduate students, primarily in the Rutherford Lab at Seattle University (SU).

LacOp

A simulation of the lactose operon, designed to help students learn and master simple genetics concepts usign the lactose operon as a basic example.

Evo

A simluation of evolution in snowshoe hares, meant to aid students learning of simple evolutionary concepts and effects of enviroment on phenotypes.

Fase

A faux sequence mutator that generates novel mutated peptide or nucleotide sequences, given an original sequence. Meant to aid teachers.
